## How to Use These Examples

1. Log in to your application
2. Navigate to "AI Landing Pages"
3. Click "New Task"
4. Copy one of the titles above into the Title field
5. Copy one of the descriptions above into the Description field
6. Click "Create & Generate"
7. Wait 10-30 seconds for the AI to generate your landing page
8. View the result and see the complete landing page

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Include concrete details about features and benefits
2. **Define Your Audience**: Clearly state who the product is for
3. **Include Numbers**: Mention pricing, user counts, time savings
4. **Value Proposition**: Explain the unique benefit clearly
5. **Feature List**: Provide 3-6 specific features
6. **Formatting**: Use clear sections and bullet points in your description

## Customizing Generated Pages

After generation, you can:
- Copy the HTML and edit it in your favorite editor
- Adjust colors by changing Tailwind classes
- Add your own branding and logo
- Modify copy to match your voice
- Add real testimonials and social proof
- Replace placeholder images with real screenshots

## Testing Different Styles

Try variations of your description to see different results:
- Emphasize different features
- Target different audiences
- Use different tone (professional vs casual)
- Focus on different benefits
- Mention different pricing strategies

The AI will generate different landing pages based on these variations!
